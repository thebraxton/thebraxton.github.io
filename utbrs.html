Under the Blood-Red Sun

Under the Blood-Red Sun is a Pearl Harbor novel, written by Graham Salisbury. Graham Salisbury's family has been in the Hawaaian Islands since the early 1800's.Graham Salisbury was born in Philidelphia and grew up in Kamuela, Hawaai. Before becoming an author in 1992, Graham Salisbury was a 'musician', most notably with The Millenium.
